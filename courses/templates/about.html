Empowering Integrity in Online Education

ExamGate was built with a clear purpose: to bridge the gap between virtual learning and exam integrity. As academic programs transition online, verification becomes a primary requirement for institutions, professional associations, and students alike.

Our platform provides a distributed oversight system where **Examiners (Conductors)**, **Teachers**, and **Candidates** work in synchronization. We give instructors complete confidence that their assessments are completed under secure and verified guidelines.

State-Of-The-Art Proctoring Technology

At the core of ExamGate is our local face and environment-tracking behavior scanning. Using modern algorithms, candidates are monitored securely from their webcams.

Instead of processing sensitive biometric video on remote databases, our system analyzes snapshots locally to preserve user privacy. Flagged actions (such as looking away, leaving the camera range, or external helper devices) are safely stored as snapshots for conductor-only audits.

Privacy First Policy

Local face detection logic keeps students' raw web feeds private while reporting key metrics only.
